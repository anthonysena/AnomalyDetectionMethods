Goal

These checks are designed to answer a practical tuning question:

  • Are the flagged anomalies stable when settings change a little?
  • Do different settings mostly preserve the same top-ranked rows?
  • Does the Gaussian model look plausible under a given preprocessing choice?

The point is not to find a single mathematically optimal setting from unlabeled data, but to find settings that are stable, interpretable, and operationally manageable.

A Practical Selection Workflow
